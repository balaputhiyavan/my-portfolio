## Overview
**EzInvoice** is a modern invoicing solution designed to help freelancers and small businesses manage their billing efficiently. Built with **Flutter** and **Firebase**, the app allows users to create, store, and track invoices seamlessly. With features like PDF generation, client management, and real-time invoice status updates, EzInvoice offers a streamlined and intuitive experience for businesses looking to simplify their invoicing process.

## ‚ùì Problem
**EzInvoice** was created to address the following challenges faced by freelancers and small businesses:
1. **Manual Invoicing Hassles:** Managing invoices through spreadsheets or paper-based methods was time-consuming and error-prone.
2. **Tracking Payments:** Difficulty in keeping track of paid and unpaid invoices, leading to delayed follow-ups and cash flow issues.
3. **Lack of Professionalism:** Without a structured invoicing system, businesses struggled to present professional, well-formatted invoices to their clients.

---

## üéØ Goal
1. Develop a simple yet powerful invoice management application for **small businesses** and **freelancers**.
2. Enable users to create, manage, and **track invoices effortlessly**.
3. Integrate a **professional PDF invoice** generation and sharing feature.
4. Provide a **client management system** to store and access client details easily.
5. Ensure smooth invoice **history tracking**, including paid and unpaid status updates.

## ‚ú® Solution

### Understanding User Needs
To ensure EzInvoice meets the needs of small business owners and freelancers, I analyzed common pain points in invoice management and identified key findings:
- **Efficiency:** Users need a fast and simple way to create and send invoices without unnecessary complexity.
- **Tracking Payments:** Clear visibility of paid and unpaid invoices is essential for better financial management.
- **Accessibility:** A cloud-based solution allows users to manage invoices from anywhere, ensuring flexibility.
- **Professionalism:** Generating well-structured PDF invoices enhances credibility with clients.

### Core Features
1. **Invoice Creation:** Easily generate professional invoices with client details, amount, date, and description.
2. **PDF Generation & Sharing:** Download invoices as PDFs or share them via email and messaging apps.
3. **Client Management:** Save and manage client details for quick invoice creation.
4. **Invoice History & Status Tracking:** View past invoices with statuses like "Paid" or "Unpaid."
5. **Firebase Integration:** Secure storage and retrieval of invoices in Firestore.
6. **User Authentication:** Google Sign-In or email/password authentication.
7. **Responsive Design:** Optimized for both mobile and desktop users.

### Design and Development Process
1. **Frontend:** Built using Flutter for a seamless and performant cross-platform experience.
2. **State Management:** Implemented MVVM architecture for better code separation and maintainability.
3. **Styling:** Designed with Flutter‚Äôs Material UI components to ensure a clean and professional look.
4. **Reusable Components:** Created modular widgets for consistency and reusability across the app.
5. **Firebase Integration:** Used Firestore for secure data storage and real-time synchronization.
6. **PDF Generation:** Implemented invoice generation using Flutter PDF libraries.
7. **Authentication:** Integrated Firebase Authentication for secure user login (Google Sign-In, email/password).

## üöå User Journey
1. **Home Screen:** A dashboard displaying invoice-related actions such as creating, viewing, and managing invoices.
2. **Invoice Creation:** A user-friendly form where users can enter client details, invoice amount, description, and due date.
3. **Invoice List:** A categorized list of all invoices, highlighting paid and unpaid statuses for easy tracking.
4. **Client Management:** A dedicated section to store and manage client details for faster invoice generation.
5. **Invoice History:** A record of all past invoices with filtering and search functionality.
6. **Invoice Preview & Sharing:** Users can generate PDFs of invoices and share them via email, WhatsApp, or other platforms.
7. **Settings Page:** Allows customization of invoice templates, company details, and user preferences.
